## What this is

The State of AI-Led Growth is an independent benchmark survey built by [GrowthX](https://growthx.ai) to measure the real gap between how AI-forward B2B marketing teams *think* they are — and what they're actually doing.

Most AI research asks what tools you use. This one goes deeper: budget allocation, AI spend visibility, content operations, channel strategy, and whether AI search engines can actually find you as a vendor.

The findings will be published as a free report.

---

## The thesis

There's a gap between self-perception and reality in AI adoption. Teams rate themselves highly, then describe workflows that are fragmented, manual, or campaign-driven rather than systemic. This benchmark is designed to quantify that gap — by role, company stage, team size, and budget.

## The survey

**27 questions across 7 sections:**

1. About You — role, company stage, team size
2. Adoption & Maturity — self-rated AI adoption (1–10) and what that actually looks like
3. Use Cases & Tools — what AI is being used for, what it costs, what's next
4. Content & Growth Operations — content strategy, budget, execution, and AI's impact on output
5. Results, Expectations & Tensions — where AI is delivering and where it's creating friction
6. AI Visibility & AEO — whether GTM teams have any strategy for how AI talks about them
7. Future Intent — where teams are headed with AI in the next 12 months

**Completion time:** ~8 minutes  
**Cost:** Free  
**Report:** All completers receive early access to the full findings
