The Project

What it is.

A 25-question survey for GTM operators, growth leads, founders, and marketing ops professionals. Five minutes to complete. The result: the first practitioner-sourced benchmark on AI-led growth, publishing June 2, 2026 — the same day ContentOS goes to market.

The thesis: AI adoption is high. AI visibility is not. And most teams have no system for turning content into compounding growth. The survey proves both gaps with real data from 500 practitioners, segmented by role and company stage so every finding is useful.

The gate is an email address. Every person who downloads the report and isn't already on our list becomes a subscriber.

The survey

25 questions across 6 categories: Adoption, Use Cases and Tools, Content Operations, Results and Tensions, AI Visibility, and Future Intent. The new Content Operations category directly maps to the gaps ContentOS closes.

The report

A free benchmark report gated behind email. Co-launches with ContentOS on June 2. Fuels 3–4 newsletter issues, a LinkedIn data series, a live workshop, and warm pipeline for ContentOS conversations through the rest of 2026.
